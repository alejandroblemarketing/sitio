About

Marketing that gets seen, teams that sell

I'm Alejandro Blé, a marketing consultant, keynote speaker and corporate trainer. For over twelve years I've helped sales teams and professionals stop being invisible: to build a personal brand, produce digital content with intent, and turn that visibility into measurable sales results.

I use real estate as a proof of concept, not as a limit.
